Steps in Requesting:

STEP 1

Click "Request" on the navigation bar.

STEP 2

Choose a file you want to request, you can specify specialized files that are not included on the available documents. Just click "Specialized Documents".

STEP 3

Fill-up the Request Form and click "Submit".

STEP 4

Check the calendar for the reception date and time.

STEP 5

Check your email for the printable "Receipt".

STEP 6

Go to the TCU Registrar's Office, show your receipt, and receive the hard copy of your requested document.
