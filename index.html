About Us

The main function of the website is to create an easier yet more effective and efficient requisition system for Taguig City University Registrar's office. This project focuses on innovating the requisition system of the university, something simple yet distinctive. It gives attention to an old mechanism, the flaws of that mechanism, and the potential of that mechanism in terms of development and innovation. In our educational system, the accessibility of records and files is very much important. That specific context makes this project versatile and relevant; this project shall help the students utilize the opportunities given to them by simply providing them with the requirements they need in the most convenient way. Creating an online requesting system can lessen unnecessary travel and time consumption. The existing system of the registrar's office in terms of handling requisitions shall become more productive because of the enhanced accessibility and faster mechanisms of the website. It will also lessen the errors and inaccuracy of the system, letting the staff use their time and effort efficiently to produce more work. The information provided to the requesters shall let them utilize the service and give feedback statements regarding the quality of the service of the registrar's office. These responses shall help the developers improve the system constantly to meet the service demand and increase the efficiency and quality of the work.
